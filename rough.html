Frequently Asked Questions

General

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary. The coaches, officers, and trainers will work with all skill levels.

Do I need to attend all practices?

You do not need to attend every practice. However, we encourage you to attend as many as possible in order to keep up with the drills and techniques. Also, individuals tend to see quicker results the more practices they attend, and we want you to get your money’s worth!

If I don’t attend UMass Amherst, can I still join the club?

As long as you pay your semester dues and can gain access to practices. If you’re looking to compete as a non-UMass Amherst affiliate, please discuss further with the coaches.

Equipment

What equipment do I need?

Water bottle, athletic clothing, sneakers, and boxing handwraps. The club offers shared jump ropes, boxing gloves, and pads. If you already have your own, feel free to bring them.

Where do I get boxing handwraps?

You can rent boxing handwraps at the equipment desk in the Campus Recreation Center for free. You can also purchase your own at various retail stores or place an order through the club for $5/pair (please reach out to a club officer if you want to purchase via the club). We recommend the 180” handwraps.

Do I need a mouth guard?

Club members only need a fitted mouth guard if they engage in sparring practices. Fitted = Boil + Bite mouth guards. Only buy a top mouth guard, not a double mouth guard.

Competition Team

Does the club compete?

We do! We compete in the National Collegiate Boxing Association (NCBA, https://www.ncbaboxing.org/) against other colleges, universities, and military academies. There are competitions in both the Fall and Spring semesters. The NCBA Regional and National tournaments are held in the Spring semester. Any students interested in competing must be approved by the coaches.

How do I get involved in the competition team?

Communicate with the coaches that you're interested in competing. The coaches will evaluate your skills and determine if you are ready for competition.

What are the competition requirements?

Apart from the rigorous training regimen, student athletes must maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA. Additionally, annual registration with USA boxing, an annual physical exam, and completion of the UMass Boxing student athlete contract are required. Coaches will notify student athletes when they need to pursue all documents necessary for competition.
