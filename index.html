Overview of the Club

The UMass Boxing Club provides an inclusive environment for all students interested in the sport. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, we focus on fitness, proper boxing techniques, and building self-discipline.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to promote physical and mental well-being through boxing. We foster an environment that encourages discipline, respect, and camaraderie while empowering individuals to surpass their limits.
