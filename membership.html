Membership

How to Join

Becoming a member of the UMass Boxing Club is simple! Just follow the steps below:

  1. Attend one of our open sessions for new members. No sign-ups required. All levels are welcome.
  2. Bring water, comfortable clothes, sneakers, boxing handwraps (available to rent), and boxing gloves (if you have them).
  3. Sign the RSO liability waiver and submit it to the club secretary before participating in practice.
  4. If you enjoyed the practice, pay for a boxing club membership to continue attending practices (details below).

The membership fees cover your participation in training sessions, equipment usage, and entry into club events:

Membership Benefits

  • Access to all weekly training sessions and sparring events.
  • Use of club equipment (boxing gloves, wraps, etc.).
  • Opportunity to compete in intercollegiate boxing tournaments.
  • Exclusive invitations to club social events and competitions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I don't skip the month?

We charge you a monthly fee if you don't skip the month.

How do I sign up to be a member?

Simply attend a training session and pay the membership fee.

Do I have to place an order every month?

No, you are free to skip any months you'd like.

What equipment do I need to join?

Bring water, athletic clothes, sneakers, and boxing handwraps.

How much does membership cost?

Our semester membership costs $50.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, but there are no refunds for cancellations after payment.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary. We welcome all skill levels.

What happens if I miss a practice?

Missing a practice is okay, but we encourage regular attendance to improve your skills.

Can I attend without joining?

You can attend one free session before deciding if you'd like to join.
